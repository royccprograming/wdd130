Cusco

Cusco is a city in southeastern Peru located on the eastern slopes of the Andes Mountains, in the Huatanay River basin, a tributary of the Vilcanota River. It is the capital of the Cusco Department and, as declared in the Peruvian Constitution, is the country's "historical capital."
